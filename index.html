Hi! I am Theo, born and raised in Larisa, Greece, currently living in Madrid, Spain, while previously lived in Prague, Czech Republic for 10 years. I work for Jobandtalent as Platform Engineer.

I am a heavy Free and Open Source user, contributor and developer. My main areas of interest are infrastructure administration and automation. I've been a user and contributor on configuration management, orchestration and other DevOps technologies for more than 10 years already; before they started being modern and cool :) Thus I am always interested in learning new infrastructure technologies, languages and products.

Over the years I have contributed to various FOSS projects, mainly as system administrator, but also as developer, packager, conference speaker/organizer and documentation translator. Most notably:

  • openSUSE, as part of the openSUSE Heroes team. I also helped on the organization of various openSUSE Conferences.
  • Gentoo, as part of the Infrastructure team. I have also participated to the Google Summer of Code program, once as a student and twice as a mentor, for Gentoo related projects.

I like to spend my free time on improving my czech and spanish language skills, movies, travelling and music (at home or going to concerts). I listen mainly to 90s rap. I always tend to visit record stores on my travels, as I'm also a vinyl collector.
